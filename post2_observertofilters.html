Observers vs Estimators vs Filters

For an outsider or if you are new to the field, the terms “observer”, “estimator” and “filter” often seem similar. Even if you are familiar with the field, it is easy to get confused with the terms or might take a minute to differentiate them. At the base level, they are all used to identify what the state of a system is.
